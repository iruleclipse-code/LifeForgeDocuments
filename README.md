# Privacy Policy for LifeForge

**Last Updated:** March 4, 2026

## 1. Introduction

LifeForge ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the LifeForge mobile application ("the App"). The App helps users build positive habits through gamification, tracking activities as RPG-style stats and quests.

## 2. Age Requirement

LifeForge is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the App. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

## 3. Data Collection & Storage

### Local Data
All core game data (stats, quests, streaks, settings) is stored locally on your device by default. Free users' data remains entirely on-device.

### Cloud Data
When you create an account or use premium features, an anonymous account is automatically created via Supabase (our cloud provider). This stores:
- Game progress (level, XP, stats, streaks, coins)
- Display name and avatar selection (if set)
- Subscription status

Premium subscribers additionally receive cloud backup, which stores your full game save to prevent data loss across devices.

### Notification Permissions
The App may request permission to send daily reminder notifications. These are scheduled locally on your device and no notification data is sent to our servers.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

- **Supabase** (supabase.com) — Cloud database, user authentication, and serverless functions. Data is stored on servers in the EU (Ireland). Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
- **RevenueCat** (revenuecat.com) — Manages subscriptions and in-app purchases securely. We do not have direct access to your payment details (credit card numbers, billing addresses). RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
- **Anthropic Claude API** (anthropic.com) — Powers the AI Activity Logger feature. Only the activity text you type is sent to the API, without personal identifiers, profile information, or device data attached. Anthropic Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
- **Apple App Store / Google Play Store** — Processes all payments for subscriptions and in-app purchases according to their respective terms.

## 5. AI Activity Logging Feature

LifeForge uses a Natural Language Processing feature powered by the Claude API to translate your typed activities into game stats. Activity text snippets are sent to the API without personal identifiers, profile information, or local device data attached. The AI does not store your inputs or learn from them between sessions.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is shared only with the essential service providers listed above, solely for core App functionality.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Local data** is retained on your device until you uninstall the App or use the "Reset Character" option in Settings.
- **Cloud data** is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, cloud data is permanently removed within 30 days.
- **AI processing data** (activity text sent to Claude API) is not stored by us or Anthropic after processing.

## 8. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You can delete all local and cloud data at any time through Settings > "Reset Character" in the App. If you no longer have the App installed, you can request permanent account and data deletion by emailing **jfgomes1991@gmail.com** with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." Requests are processed within 30 days.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 10. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: **jfgomes1991@gmail.com**
