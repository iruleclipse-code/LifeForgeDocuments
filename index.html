Privacy Policy for LifeForge

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

1. Introduction

LifeForge ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the LifeForge mobile application. Our app is designed to help you track your habits and level up your life securely.

2. Data Collection, Cloud Saves, and Subscriptions

  • Free Users (Local Storage): For standard users, all game data, stats, and logs are stored strictly locally on your device.
  • Premium Users (Cloud Sync): If you subscribe to LifeForge Premium, your save data is securely backed up to our cloud database to prevent data loss. This acts as your user account.
  • Subscriptions (RevenueCat): We use RevenueCat to securely manage our Premium subscriptions and in-app purchases. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat processes your transaction and purchase history securely. We do not have direct access to your credit card information.

3. AI Activity Logging Feature

LifeForge uses a Natural Language Processing feature (powered by the Claude API) to translate your typed activities into game stats.

  • When you type an activity (e.g., "smashed legs today"), that specific text snippet is sent securely to the API.
  • No Personal Identifiers: This text is sent anonymously. No personal identifiers, profile information, or local device data are attached to these requests.

4. Data Sharing and Sales

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with essential service providers (RevenueCat for subscriptions, our cloud provider for Premium backups) strictly to provide the app's core functionality.

5. Account and Data Deletion Rights

You have the absolute right to request the complete deletion of your data and account at any time, in compliance with Google Play policies.

  • In-App Deletion: You can instantly delete all local data (and cloud data, if Premium) by navigating to the Settings menu and tapping "Reset Character."
  • Web/Email Deletion Request: If you no longer have the app installed, you can request the complete and permanent deletion of your account and all associated cloud data by emailing us at jfgomes1991@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request." We will permanently delete your data within 30 days.
