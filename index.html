Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

1. Introduction

LifeForge ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the LifeForge mobile application ("the App"). The App helps users build positive habits through gamification, tracking activities as RPG-style stats and quests.

2. Age Requirement

LifeForge is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the App. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

3. Data Collection & Storage

Local Data

All core game data (stats, quests, streaks, achievements, settings, activity logs) is stored locally on your device by default. Free users' data remains entirely on-device.

Cloud Data

When you use the App, an anonymous account is automatically created via Supabase (our cloud provider). This stores:

  • Game progress (level, XP, stats, streaks, coins)
  • Display name, username, avatar selection, and friend code (if set)
  • Subscription status
  • Achievement IDs (for display on social profiles)

Premium subscribers additionally receive cloud backup, which stores your full game save to prevent data loss across devices.

Social & Guild Data

If you use social features, the following data is stored on our servers:

  • Follow/follower relationships
  • Guild membership, guild chat messages, and guild activity
  • VS challenge data (challenge type, progress, completion status)
  • Weekly XP totals (for leaderboards)
  • Accountability partner connections

Guild chat messages are subject to a profanity filter. Messages containing inappropriate language are rejected and not stored.

Push Notification Data

If you enable push notifications, your device's push token (Expo Push Token) is stored on our servers to deliver notifications. Push tokens are associated with your anonymous user ID and are deleted when you disable notifications or delete your account. Notification content may be personalised using AI (see Section 5).

Language Preference

Your chosen language preference is stored locally on your device and in your game save. It is not transmitted separately to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — Cloud database, user authentication, edge functions, and scheduled tasks. Data is stored on servers in the EU (Ireland). Supabase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) — Manages subscriptions and in-app purchases securely. We do not have direct access to your payment details (credit card numbers, billing addresses). RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API (anthropic.com) — Powers the AI Activity Logger and AI-personalised push notifications. Only activity text and anonymised game context (stat levels, streak count) are sent — no personal identifiers, profile information, or device data. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Expo Push Notifications (expo.dev) — Delivers push notifications to your device. Expo Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — Processes all payments for subscriptions and in-app purchases according to their respective terms.

5. AI Features

AI Activity Logger

LifeForge uses a Natural Language Processing feature powered by the Claude API to translate your typed activities into game stats and XP. Activity text snippets are sent to the API without personal identifiers, profile information, or local device data attached. The AI does not store your inputs or learn from them between sessions.

AI-Personalised Notifications

If you enable push notifications, the App may use AI to generate personalised motivational messages based on your anonymised game context (e.g., current streak, stat levels, recent achievements). No personal information (name, email, location) is included in the AI prompt. Generated messages are sent once and not stored.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is shared only with the essential service providers listed above, solely for core App functionality.

Social features allow other users to see your display name, avatar, class, level, achievement badges, and weekly XP. This information is visible only to users you follow or who follow you, and to fellow guild members.

7. Data Retention

  • Local data is retained on your device until you uninstall the App or use the "Reset Character" option in Settings.
  • Cloud data (profile, stats, social connections, guild data) is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, cloud data is permanently removed within 30 days.
  • Guild chat messages are retained for the lifetime of the guild. If you leave a guild, your messages remain visible to guild members.
  • AI processing data (activity text and notification prompts sent to Claude API) is not stored by us or Anthropic after processing.
  • Push tokens are deleted when you disable notifications or delete your account.

8. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You can delete all local and cloud data at any time through Settings > "Reset Character" in the App. If you no longer have the App installed, you can request permanent account and data deletion by emailing support@lifeforge.app with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." Requests are processed within 30 days.

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR and CCPA), you may also have the right to access, correct, or port your personal data. Contact us at the email above to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at:
support@lifeforge.app
