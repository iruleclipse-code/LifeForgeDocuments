Privacy Policy for LifeForge

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

1. Introduction

LifeForge ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the LifeForge mobile application. Our app is designed to help you track your habits and level up your life with maximum privacy.

2. Data Collection and Storage

  • Local Storage Only: All of your user data—including your profile, stats, activity logs, and achievements—is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain user accounts, and we do not store your personal data on any external servers.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or any third-party data collection tools.

3. AI Activity Logging Feature

LifeForge uses a Natural Language Processing feature (powered by the Claude API) to translate your typed activities into game stats.

  • When you type an activity (e.g., "Ran 5k today"), that specific text snippet is sent securely to the API to determine the appropriate stat rewards.
  • No Personal Identifiers: This text is sent anonymously. No personal identifiers, profile information, or local device data are attached to these requests.

4. Data Sharing and Sales

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Because your data is stored exclusively on your device, we do not have access to it to share it.

5. User Deletion Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can delete all your data instantly by clearing the app's cache in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.
